# Migration Plan

## Recommended Build Order (Dependency Hierarchy)

1. **Phase 1 - Core & Auth:** Users, Roles, Permissions, Auth Module.
2. **Phase 2 - Organization:** Colleges, Departments, Programs.
3. **Phase 3 - Academics & People:** Employees, Students, Mentors, Batches.
4. **Phase 4 - Transactions:** Applications, Fees, Payments, Wallet.
5. **Phase 5 - Learning & Execution:** LMS, Assessments, Submissions, Tasks, Attendance.
6. **Phase 6 - Support & Comms:** Helpdesk, Notifications, Email, Escalations.
7. **Phase 7 - Analytics & Misc:** Analytics, Export, KPI, Feedback, Reports.
